On this episode of *Hour of Excellence*, host Mrs. Bernice Presley welcomes Mrs. Tomi S. Brown and Mr. Kent Banner for an informative discussion about elections, civic engagement, and the important role of the Supervisor of Elections Office in Columbia County.

Mrs. Brown explains how her office works year-round to ensure elections are conducted fairly, accurately, and securely. Listeners learn about voter registration, vote-by-mail procedures, polling locations, election integrity, and the many resources available to voters through the Elections Office. \
\
Mr. Banner discusses election security and integrity. 

**About Our Guest:**

Mrs. Tomi S. Brown serves as the Columbia County Supervisor of Elections, overseeing voter registration, election administration, vote-by-mail services, poll worker training, and election results reporting for Columbia County, Florida.\
\
Mr. Kent Banner serves as the Columbia County Assistant Supervisor of Elections. He manages computer systems for tabulation, auditing and voter registration.
